Aidan's Best Anime List

My Top 3 Animes of All Time

One Piece

By far the best world building, the plots interesting and very detailed. Great music and has many great life lessons. Covers real world problems.

Hunter x Hunter

Good world building, best plot I've seen and is extrememly detailed Great music, animation, and has great life lessons. Makes you question reality

Death Note

Very interesting and the most detailed plot I've seen. Probably the best show to introduce someone into anime.
